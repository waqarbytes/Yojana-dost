General Questions

Yojana Dost is a comprehensive digital platform that provides access to over 400 government schemes from central and state governments. It helps citizens discover, understand, and apply for various welfare schemes and benefits they are eligible for.

Yes, Yojana Dost is completely free to use. All features including scheme search, AI chatbot assistance, and application tracking are available at no cost to all citizens.

Our database is updated regularly to ensure you have access to the latest scheme information. We monitor government announcements and update our records as soon as new schemes are launched or existing ones are modified.

While you can browse schemes without an account, creating a profile allows you to bookmark schemes, track applications, and receive personalized recommendations. Account creation is optional but recommended for a better experience.

Government Schemes

Currently, we have over 400 government schemes from both central and state governments covering various categories including health, education, agriculture, women empowerment, housing, and more.

You can use our search feature, browse by categories, filter by state, or use our AI chatbot to find schemes relevant to your profile. The chatbot can provide personalized recommendations based on your requirements.

Yes, we include both central government schemes that are applicable nationwide and state-specific schemes from all Indian states and union territories.

For each scheme, we provide details including eligibility criteria, benefits, required documents, application process, deadlines, and direct links to official application portals.

Applications

Yojana Dost serves as an information portal. While we provide comprehensive scheme details and guidance, actual applications must be submitted through official government portals. We provide direct links to these official application portals.

Application tracking is done through the respective government portals where you submitted your application. We provide links to these tracking systems and guidance on how to check your application status.

Required documents vary by scheme but commonly include Aadhaar card, income certificate, residence proof, bank account details, and category certificates (if applicable). Specific requirements are listed for each scheme.

Yes, you can apply for multiple schemes simultaneously as long as you meet the eligibility criteria for each scheme. However, some schemes may have restrictions on concurrent applications.

Eligibility

Each scheme page contains detailed eligibility criteria including age, income, residence, category, and other requirements. You can also use our AI chatbot to get personalized eligibility assessment.

Central government schemes are available nationwide, while state schemes are specific to respective states. Use our state filter to find schemes applicable to your location.

While some schemes are category-specific (SC/ST/OBC/EWS), many schemes are available to all citizens regardless of category. Eligibility criteria are clearly mentioned for each scheme.

If you don't meet all criteria for a specific scheme, explore other schemes in the same category or use our AI chatbot to find alternative schemes that match your profile.

Technical Support

Yes, Yojana Dost is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktops. You can access all features seamlessly across different devices.

Our AI chatbot is trained on our comprehensive database of 400+ schemes. It uses natural language processing to understand your queries and provide relevant scheme information, eligibility details, and application guidance.

Yojana Dost works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and mobile browsers. We recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser for the best experience.

For technical issues, you can contact our support team through the Contact Us page, file a grievance, or use the AI chatbot for immediate assistance. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Account Management

Click on the "Profile" link in the navigation menu to access the account creation page. You'll need to provide basic information like name, email, and phone number to create your account.

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

Yes, you can update your profile information anytime by going to your profile page and clicking the "Edit Profile" button. Keep your information updated for better scheme recommendations.

Yes, we use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.
