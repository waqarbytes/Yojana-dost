Empowering Citizens Through Digital Governance

Yojana Dost is a comprehensive digital platform designed to bridge the gap between government schemes and citizens. We believe that every citizen deserves easy access to government benefits, schemes, and services that can transform their lives.

Our mission is to simplify the complex landscape of government schemes, making it easier for citizens to discover, understand, and apply for benefits they're entitled to. With over 400 government schemes from central and state governments, we serve as your trusted companion in navigating the world of public welfare.

Our Mission

To democratize access to government schemes and services by creating a user-friendly, comprehensive platform that empowers every citizen to benefit from public welfare programs. We strive to eliminate barriers and simplify the journey from awareness to application.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and comprehensive digital gateway for government schemes, ensuring that no citizen is left behind in accessing the benefits they deserve. We envision a future where technology bridges the gap between governance and citizens.
